Instructions:

This app is designed to utilize OAuth2.0 and OpendID to issue an authorization token (to access your google profile info) and a JWT (to identify who is creating/querying datastore data) The app is going to perform the following:

  1. After you click "Log In with Google", you will be redirected to log in to your google profile.
  2. Once you log in, you have authorized me to access your public information. I will use an authorization token to query the Google People API.
  3. I will then query your name information from the Google People API and display a page showing your first name, last name, your unique datastore ID, and the JWT token that was issued as part of the authentication.
  4. The backend will create a user account for you within datastore if this is your first time logging in.
  5. You can now use the JWT displayed to create rentals and query rentals. The rental/user relationship will be stored within datastore.
  6. Begin by clicking "Log In With Google" below.
