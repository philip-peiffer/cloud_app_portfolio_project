Instructions:

This app is designed to utilize OAuth2.0 and OpendID to issue an authorization token (to access your google profile info) and a JWT (to identify who is creating/querying datastore data) The app is going to perform the following:

  1. After you click "Allow Google Access", you will be redirected to log in to your google profile.
  2. Once you log in, you have authorized me to access your public information. I will use an authorization token to query the Google People API.
  3. I will then query your name information from the Google People API and display a page showing your first name, last name, and the JWT token that was issued as part of the authentication.
  4. Copy the JWT. This JWT can be used to create/delete boats from the datastore by calling the API endpoints.
  5. Begin by clicking "Allow Google Access" below.
